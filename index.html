About Us

Founded in 2024 by Khanyisile Katlego Zwane, a young visionary woman, KAYYSTYLES_WEAR embodies the spirit of hustle, entrepreneurship and bold self expression. Rooted in the streets of Soweto, our brand creates authentic streetwear that celebrates individuality, resilience and the drive to carve your own path. MORE than just fashion, we believe in building strong foundations both in style and in community, using our platform to support and uplift those in need. EVERY piece tells a story of ambition, creativity and the power of giving back, making KAYYSTYLES_WEAR NOT JUST a label but a movement.
